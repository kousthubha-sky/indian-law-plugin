# Voluntary Standards

These standards are not legally mandated for most private entities, but:
- Demonstrate "reasonable security" intent (reduces DPDP and IT Act liability)
- May be required by enterprise clients in procurement
- Differentiate the product/service in competitive bids
- Some (IS 17802) are mandatory for government portals

---

### OBL-STD-01 — IS 17428: Data Privacy Assurance Standard
- **requirement (voluntary):** IS 17428:2021/2022 is India's BIS standard for privacy management systems — the Indian equivalent of ISO 27701 (privacy extension to ISO 27001). Certification demonstrates structured privacy controls: DPIA process, privacy governance, vendor management, incident response, awareness training.
- **applies_when:** Entities seeking to demonstrate DPDP Act compliance readiness proactively
- **evidence_hints:** IS 17428 audit report; certification document from a BIS-accredited auditor; `privacy-management-plan.md` or DPIA templates in project docs; privacy training records
- **severity:** low
- **remediation:** If pursuing certification: engage a BIS-accredited audit body. Core controls to implement: privacy policy + notice, consent management, data mapping, DPIA, vendor/sub-processor agreements, incident response, awareness training, internal audit.
- **citation:** IS 17428 (BIS); DPDP Act 2023 §8 (reasonable security basis)

### OBL-STD-02 — ISO 27001: Information Security Management System
- **requirement (voluntary):** ISO 27001:2022 certification demonstrates a structured Information Security Management System (ISMS): risk assessment, security controls, access management, incident response, business continuity, supplier security. Not mandated by Indian law for most entities, but widely required by enterprise/government clients.
- **applies_when:** Entities with enterprise/government clients or high data volumes
- **evidence_hints:** ISO 27001 certificate in project docs; ISMS policy document; Statement of Applicability (SoA); annual internal audit report; risk register
- **severity:** low
- **remediation:** Engage an ISO 27001 accredited certification body. Key controls: access control policy, encryption policy, incident management, business continuity plan, supplier agreements, annual internal audit, management review. Certification valid 3 years with annual surveillance audits.
- **citation:** ISO/IEC 27001:2022; IT Act 2000 §43A (IS 17428/ISO 27001 as benchmark for "reasonable security")
